Who We Are

Two Decades of Crafting Exceptional Floor Experiences

Established in 2000 in Telangana, Adarsh Technology Services has spent over two decades mastering the art and science of industrial flooring. We are pioneers in decorative concrete floors and wall finishes across Hyderabad and Secunderabad.

From luxury residences to large-scale commercial installations, our team brings unmatched technical skill, aesthetic sensibility, and a commitment to finishes that endure decades of daily life.

What We Do

Our Craft, Our Services

01 ✨

Floor Sanding & Polishing

Bringing tired floors back to life with professional diamond polishing and sanding. We restore natural stone, hardwood, and concrete surfaces to mirror-like perfection.

02 🎨

Decorative Concrete

Custom decorative concrete solutions for homes and commercial spaces. Stampings, stencils, colour hardeners, and exposed aggregate finishes crafted for lasting beauty.

03 🏠

Home Décor Flooring

Bespoke flooring solutions designed to complement your interiors. From micro-topping to metallic epoxy, we create conversation-starting floors for luxury residences.

04 🏭

Industrial Flooring

High-performance industrial floors engineered for heavy traffic, chemical resistance, and extreme durability. Warehouses, factories, and showrooms — built to last.

05 🧱

Wall Finishes

Textured plaster, stone cladding, and decorative wall coatings that elevate any interior. Our wall finish specialists transform flat surfaces into architectural features.

06 🔧

Repair & Restoration

Expert crack repair, grinding, levelling, and concrete restoration. We diagnose and solve structural and aesthetic floor problems with lasting solutions.
